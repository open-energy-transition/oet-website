Free Monthly PyPSA Support for System Operators

Open Energy Transition is pleased to offer free monthly support sessions exclusively for European transmission system operators exploring PyPSA and open-source energy modeling.

What you'll get:

  • Direct access to OET's PyPSA experts (1-hour sessions)
  • Answers to your questions about PyPSA-Eur, Snakemake, and implementation
  • Guidance on IT infrastructure and self-hosting requirements
  • Understanding of model capabilities and use cases
  • A valuable opportunity to network with other TSOs and share experiences together
These collaborative sessions bring together transmission system operators to learn from both OET experts and each other's experiences.
